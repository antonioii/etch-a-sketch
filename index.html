Instructions

Click in the plus or minus symbol in order to show or hide the instructions and the tool box content.

Click in a tool image to activate the choosen tool. Each tool has an interrogation mark aside which explains the functionality of that specific tool.

The canvas is where you must click and drag to paint. Below the canvas, you can chose the size sample (how many squares your canvas will be using).

Click Clear all to clean all the canvas drawings.
