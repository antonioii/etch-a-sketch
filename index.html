Instructions

To paint, click and move inside the canvas field.

Click in the plus/minus green symbol in the instructions or tools area to show or hide the the instructions/tool box content.

Click in a tool image to activate the choosen tool. Each tool has an interrogation mark aside which explains the functionality of that specific tool.

The canvas is where you must click and drag to paint. Above the canvas itself, you can chose the size sample (how many rectangles will be displaying to paint).

Click Clear all to clean all the canvas drawings.
