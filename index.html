Instructions

To paint, click and move inside the canvas field.

Click in the plus/minus green symbol in the instructions or tools area to show or hide the instructions/tool box content.

Click in a tool image to activate the choosen tool. When a tool is activated, you will see its image bigger than when is not activated.

Each tool has an interrogation mark aside. Put the mouse over the interrogation to see the functionality of that specific tool.

The canvas is where you must click and drag to paint. Above the canvas itself, you can chose the size sample (how many rectangles will be displaying to paint) and a button to paint all the canvas with a basic color.

Click Clear all to clean all the canvas drawings.
